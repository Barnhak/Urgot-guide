✦ Urgot Keystones
Main Keystone — 99% of games
Press the Attack (PTA)
PTA is the main keystone you will use in 99% of your games while playing Urgot. It gives high damage, trade power and pressure — it synergizes perfectly with your W and passive to shred enemies fast.
Niche Keystone
Deathfire Touch
Deathfire Touch will only be useful in matchups where you can auto or trade freely like Heimerdinger. It provides burn damage on hit which helps sustain poke in difficult lanes.

Sorcery Tree — Secondary (Standard)
Triple Tonic + Approach Velocity
Row 1Triple Tonic
Row 2Approach Velocity
Triple Tonic gives 3 pots at start + ability haste. Approach Velocity lets you close the gap when you slow with Q — essential for chasing and executing enemies.
Sorcery Tree — Secondary (Vs Olaf)
Nimbus Cloak + Celerity
Row 1Nimbus Cloak
Row 2Celerity
Only vs Olaf. Nimbus Cloak gives insane MS when you cast a summoner spell — essential to escape his R. Celerity boosts your bonus MS by 7%.
