Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand. And in secondary runes, Triple Tonic and Approach Velocity.

Summoners spells

You can go Ignite — you need to OS Gwen if you fight her and her passive heals so much you need some anti heal.

Starter item
Doran's Blade
Standard start

How to play VS

Level 1 try go behind Gwen and fight her, just E + PTA and disengage. If she starts Q she will out trade you if you continue and heal. And if she skills E just proc PTA and zone her from wave while poking her with legs.

Try not letting her stack her Q and when Q / E is on CD or has 1/2 stack just poke her. If her Q has 2+ stacks try dodging her incoming Q → E. If Gwen has her E up you can't E except with Flash or she will react to it and if you fail your E you die. If Gwen wastes E and W you can OS her at 75% HP with Flash E + R.

If Gwen W and stands on the edge of her W your E will go through her and she will destroy you but if she just W and is at 70% HP you can just E and destroy her.

If you destroy Gwen — even though she's good in lane — you will be very useful but Gwen is really good at 3+ items and outscales you very hard even though in 1v1 you can win if you flip her (E).
