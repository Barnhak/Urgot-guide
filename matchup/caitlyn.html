Early game: Need to get behind Caitlyn from the brushes and fight level 1 before she gets her E dash. It will be hard to hit E due to her range, so if she doesn't walk into flip range just E to gapclose/shield and auto to push her into the river to deny EXP.

How to trade: Due to her insane range, you can only look for Q poke, no auto attacks. Poke with Q as much as you can, then look to one shot with E Flash Ignite at ~80%HP, or even higher depending on your items. Unless she really misposistions, don't try to E normally at her. She can also dodge it with her own E if that's up still. Try to be patient and wait for level 6, so you can guarantee kill with R + E Flash + Ignite.

VERY IMPORTANT TO CONTROL THE BRUSHES! Helps avoid getting perma autoed and try to get behind/flank her after 6 for fights. Caitlyn also stacks her headshot 2x as fast in the bush, so it's important you don't let her control them. Usually have to play very respectfully if your Flash is down, but after 6 you can look to Q → E to gapclose → R + ignite run her down if she's a bit chunked.

What to watch out for: You'll have to give CS to preserve HP, that's fine. Don't let her auto you for free perma and poke you out. Watch out for the traps, especially in the brushes or near your tower.

Tips: Matchup is tough 1v1, but she is a very bad sidelaner and offers nothing for the enemy comp later on. Focus on going even and then being more effective in sidelane later where you can run her down, or teamfights where you're tankier. Steelcaps rush very strong, especially if they have 2+ AD. If you get on top of Caitlyn she just dies. Her Q does reduced dmg per unit hit so try to stand behind your minions if you can't dodge it.
