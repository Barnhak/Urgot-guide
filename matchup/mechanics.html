Urgot Guide

Champion Mechanics

Kit, power spikes & spell maxing

Urgot is the only ranged juggernaut in the game. He plays top and is a beast in both early and late game — huge damage output and a high health pool. On this page we go through the kit, how to play Urgot, some tricks and more.

For more detail, visit the Urgot Wiki page ↗

✦ The Kit
PassiveEchoing Flame

Each Urgot leg has a shotgun covering a 60° angle. Auto attacks cause the shotgun to shoot in a cone dealing AD damage scaling with Urgot's base level AD and enemy max HP. Cooldown from 30s early to 2.5s late, per leg.

How to use

Play around your legs and save them to poke your opponent. If enemies are CC'd, rotate around them to apply max damage. While farming, proc a leg that won't affect the wave and use that same leg to last hit minions.

QCorrosive Charge

Urgot launches a missile which explodes after 0.3s and slows the target for 1.5s.

How to use
  1. Poke the opponent to lower their HP then all in. Q and proc PTA then disengage.
  2. Slow the opponent to engage or disengage.
  3. Farm from a safe distance.
WPurge

Purge puts Urgot in a state where he can't auto but fires at the nearest target at 3 attacks per second. Other spells mark champions — Purge focuses them instead of nearest enemy. While in Purge, Urgot ignores minion collision and is slowed by 125 points. At max rank 0 mana cost and no CD.

How to use

Used to farm with high DPS or after marking a target to deal huge damage with PTA. Don't grid too much due to the slow — some champions will turn you (Lethal Tempo, Conqueror users).

EDisdain

Urgot shields himself and dashes forward. The first target hit is bumped and thrown behind Urgot then CC'd for 1s. For the next 1.75s Urgot can do nothing.

How to use

Key spell for killing your opponent. Learn the dash range and use Q to gap close before dashing. Use it level 1 to trade even if you miss — the shield is strong at level 1.

If you hit your E you can buffer Q or R (even both) to assure they land.

RFear Beyond Death

Urgot fires a drill dealing Physical damage to the first target hit. Target is marked for 4 seconds, revealed and slowed 0–75% based on missing health. If target gets under 25% max HP, Urgot chains and drags them for 1.5s and executes them, fearing nearby enemies and slowing 75% for 1.5s.

How to use

Key spell in teamfight — the fear has huge impact. Try using E before R. If opponent is tanky, the 4s timer may be too short — hold R until you can kill them in that window.

✦ Power Spikes
1
Level 9
W Toggle + Black Cleaver
At level 9 you unlock the toggle of W and usually complete Black Cleaver. Your first and biggest power spike — you become extremely dangerous.
2
After Level 9
Sterak's Gage
Sterak makes you really tanky and allows you to pull R more consistently in close matchups or teamfights. Your defensive cornerstone.
3
Level 13 / 16
Late Game Passive
At level 13 your passive reaches its lower cooldown (2.5s per leg) and at level 16 it deals more damage than ever — 100% base AD + 6% target max HP.
✦ Spell Maxing

Level 1 — depends on opponent. High range like Teemo, Vayne or Jayce → start Q if they don't facecheck your bush. Otherwise start E.

Level 2 — If you started Q always take E after. Else take Q or W depending on situation. If you need to push or can kill/dive with E Flash, or want to trade with a stable wave → take W. If target is low due to a hard trade level 1 → take Q to apply pressure.

Goal is to max W at level 9. Triple Tonic: 3 in Q, 5 in W, 1 in E, 1 in R. Else 2 in Q, 5 in W, 1 in E, 1 in R. Max Q after W. Max E last.

Standard max order
E × 1→ Q or W × 1→ Other × 1→ Q × 1 (2 with tonic)→ W × 4→ Q × max→ E × max

Mandatory mechanic to learn. Unlocks properly at level 9 when W is fully maxed.

✦ Wave Management

Urgot got one of the hardest wave management of all toplane. His Legs make it really hard to not wipe waves with two auto and here's how do get your wave in order

Freeze
Keep 3-4 more minions than the enemy in front of your tower. The enemy has to walk into your range to farm.
  • After winning a trade, summon-down or kill the enemy
  • Your passive legs can break the freeze — anticipate their direction
  • Your Q range lets you CS safely while you zone
FREEZE
Slow Push
Only kill the melees, let the casters pile up. The wave grows and crashes the enemy tower fast.
  • Ideal setup before a dive or a jungler roam
  • Crash with W toggle → reset → come back with an item lead
  • Your W clears cannons in 2-3 seconds
SLOW PUSH
Fast Push
W toggle + passive legs = wave cleared in seconds. Punishes enemy TPs and generates item leads.
  • Crash then go back to base — return with an item lead
  • Best against splitpushers — crash and regroup
  • Freezing is often still more profitable if you're winning lane
FAST PUSH
Level 1 — Going Behind
Hide in your bush and wait for the enemy to walk up so you can hit them from behind with your legs.
  • Facecheck on your bush = free kill at level 2
  • Chunking 30-40% HP at level 1 makes the enemy play defensively the whole lane
LVL 1
Crash + Dive Setup
Crash a big wave under the enemy tower — their tower aggroes the minions — you dive while that happens.
  • Slow push → crash → JGL arrives = dive almost guaranteed
  • E Flash + R under tower = direct execute
  • Your E shield absorbs tower shots during the dive
DIVE
Passive Legs — Watch Out
Your cone shotguns hit minions too. While freezing, a misplaced leg pushes the wave and breaks everything.
  • Freezing: aim your legs toward the enemy minions
  • Farming: proc legs on minions you're about to last-hit
  • Trading: step back so your legs hit the enemy, not the wave
