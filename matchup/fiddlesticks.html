Early game: Get behind Fiddle lvl 1 and try to push him into the river to deny EXP. If he starts W he just dies, but most likely starts Q or E. Either one look to buffer your E thru and just trade autos.

How to trade: This matchup comes entirely down to how good your E buffer is thru his Q fear. They're the same range, so look to Q → run at him → E as soon as you're in range — usually you'll buffer E thru Q and then you just kill him. His fear doesn't knockdown/stop your E, so as long as you get it off he's cooked. You can also buffer AWAY from his fear if you want to run (jg gank). If he stands in your wave and sucks (W), just flip and kill. His E silence is tricky to dodge due to its longer range, but only the outside edge silences. If you can close the gap off Q slow he's super vulnerable.

You can play really aggro at any point during this lane if you hit flip. You can also fight him whenever, look to walk behind the wave and shove him off exp, he shouldn't be able to farm.

What to watch out for: His healing is pretty nuts if there are lots of minions around, be careful to not overcommit if you don't have Ignite/antiheal up. Good Fiddles will E (silence) → Q (fear) so you can't buffer E, so watch out for this on ganks. He'll go down 50+ CS but his CC will always make him relevant. Spam ping when he tries to roam mid, his ganks are nuts post 6. His effigy clones (passive) can BLOCK your abilities, especially R. He can place it in front to block R snipes.

Tips: Merc treads rush is really good, especially if they have AP jungle/mid. Be careful of his W sustain if you miss E/don't have Ignite or R, but otherwise this is a relatively weak pick in lane despite insane gank setup/roams — SPAM ping if he goes missing.
