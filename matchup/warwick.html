Warwick

✦ Urgot Favored 4/10

Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand. And in secondary runes, Triple Tonic and Approach Velocity to gap close and have kill pressure.

Summoners spells

Ignite is mandatory — Warwick's whole kit revolves around healing and being unkillable at low HP. Anti heal is essential to kill him.

How to play VS

Level 1 most Warwicks will try to walk up and Q you. Auto → E → Auto — they will be half HP and forced to concede prio. Wincon is E'ing level 1 as he Q's you for a big trade — maintain your spacing in this lane and never let him Q you for free.

Playing this lane is all about sneaking in Autos/Legs without letting him Q. Your autos barely outrange his Q so spacing is crucial — if he trades autos/Q you lose eventually. Use your range, push him in, poke him — but do not let him Q you. If he walks up to CS: Q → PTA/Leg combo. Be careful if he chooses to fight you — trading perma without having lethal is good for Warwick since being half HP or below is his wincon. Even if it looks good at the start, Warwick wins lane by being unkillable below 25% HP and then outsustaining you.

Don't get baited — Warwick wants to be low so he gets more damage and healing, and all his healing/tankiness is backloaded with his R/E. Also factor in his Barrier + E. You can use your E to small trade — try to flip him sideways rather than behind you and make space after so he can't Q you easily. Buy Executioners even if you have Ignite ASAP.

You hard outscale at 9/1st item and Executioners. He's turbo useless in teamfights and his wincon is cheesing lane to get fed.
