Early game: Get behind Lissandra and fight lvl 1, she's super weak. She has lowish range, so you can usually walk up to her and flip if she stops to auto/Q. If she starts Q and throws it out to slow you, just E thru it and trade autos, push her into river to deny EXP. If she doesn't throw out Q, she might have W, try to E buffer thru it. Very squishy so if she takes a trade level 1, you can E Flash lvl 2 with W to kill.

How to trade: Lissandra Q extends thru minions, so don't stand behind them — try to approach Lissandra from the side/brushes to force her to fight. 3 points Q with triple tonic, and just poke with Q while trying to dodge her Q. If she uses Q on the wave, look to go aggro Q → W PTA trade. If she's low enough, you can look for Q→E and E buffer thru her W root to force hard trades if you can get close enough, this is harder to do before merc treads/3 points Q. E buffering thru her W root is crucial to forcing longer trades, you usually need Q slow + boots to be able to get close enough fast. You want to walk a bit after Q slow when you're hasted (approach velocity) and she's slowed (Q) so you can get into E range and buffer W. If you insta E and it doesn't hit she just roots and walks away.

Her E is a pretty slow escape ability, you can usually E flash in its animation before she can get far enough. But you also need to make sure she's low enough (~50% HP) when E Flash Ring as if she lives thru the E stun she can R herself to time it out.

What to watch out for: Her gank setup is crazy with Ult/W CC, so be careful if enemy jungle is topside. Ping her roams if she goes MIA to mid lane as well, her E gives her great gank angles.

Tips: You hard outscale in sidelane later on, and the lane is pretty chill. Farm, play for Q poke, and once you get 3 points in Q + mercs you can start Q→Eing aggressively and it's hard for her to avoid that. You can E buffer her R, you'll still be stunned but you'll move farther away.
