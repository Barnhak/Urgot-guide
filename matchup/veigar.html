Early game: Very important you go super aggressive level 1, get behind him in the bush and push him away from the creeps. Veigar cannot do anything level 1. If he doesn't enter flip range, just E forwards and auto attack while denying him EXP.

How to trade: This matchup is all about dodging Veigar's Q, while poking him down with your own Q, until you can E Flash one shot at ~70%HP. As with most ranged matchups, Flash is critical otherwise you have no kill/gank pressure on him. Don't waste it! Since Veigar's E cage has a delay to spawn, he doesn't have much counterplay if you can get close the E since he'll be stunned. Only look to extend a Q into a trade if he has no Q/E cage, unless you can get close enough where he can always force you away with his E.

Take the bush! This helps avoid his poke, and also lets you wrap around behind Veigar. If you can get behind him a lot easier to force him to fight. If you manage to close the gap, you can Q → W PTA and trade. If you have enough MS and you get close, you can Q→E but you do need to be close or he can just drop the cage to zone you. If you can't get past his long range, just poke with Q — only look to Q→W PTA if he missed his Q/wastes E cage.

What to watch out for: Veigar's E Cage KNOCKS down your E, you cannot E buffer thru it! Veigar's Q passes thru 2 units, so be careful standing around low HP minions that he wants to Q stack. Be careful of his burst with R, it executes on %missing health. It's hard to all in if you start off with ~50%HP.

Tips: If enemy jg/comp has 2-3+ AP, merc treads rush is OP. If Veigar builds lost chapter/items that aren't health or armor, the matchup is significantly easier, as you can always threaten 1shot if he's ~80%HP. Focus on playing sidelane later on where Veigar is vulnerable to flanks.
