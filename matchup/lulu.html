Early game: Get behind Lulu lvl 1 in the brush and push her into the river. She always starts Q, so it will be hard to hit the flip — save E to buffer through her Q slow and trade autos. You probably won't be able to chunk her super hard, but deny her EXP in the river. If she does take a bad trade, level 2 with W you can E Flash to kill.

How to trade: Tricky matchup due to her high range + Q slow to zone you. Play slow and don't force trades until she wastes abilities. After level 1, just play for Q poke if she walks up. If she has no Q, you can look to Q→Auto PTA→disengage.

The main spike you should play for is 3 points Q (triple tonic) + Merc Treads, as that will allow you to Q→E her. You can buffer E through polymorph, you just need to get close enough to hit flip.

Try to control the brushes to avoid auto poke, and stand outside her Q range and just farm. If she E's a minion → Qs you, that's pretty unavoidable but costs 2 spells and more mana so it's fine. All-in at 50% HP with E Flash R, as her Ult HP + knockup + polymorph can easily time out your R if she doesn't die in E stun.

What to watch out for: Be careful of missing your E, as you will take a lot of damage with her Q slow + autos. Waiting until boots/3 points Q makes it much harder to miss E, especially if she has no Q to slow. Good Lulus will save their R knockup if you E first to knock it down.

Tips: You turbo outscale in sidelane/5v5 later on, she just becomes a utility support. Going even until 9 is really strong, as then you start to outfarm her very fast as she can't sidelane. Merc Treads rush is super nice, especially if they have 2-3+ AP. You can E buffer her polymorph and Ult knockup.
