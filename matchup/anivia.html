How to play VS

Phase 1
Level 1–3
Get behind her from brushes and trade hard. Buffer E through her Q stun if you can't flip.
  • Push her into river to deny EXP
  • Don't commit Flash — very hard to kill egg in time
Phase 2
Level 4–8
Q poke only — never auto into her. Bait Q stun before extending any trade.
  • Stay away from Wall range especially post-6
  • Q → run at her → sidestep to bait panic Q
  • Rush Merc Treads if 2+ AP on enemy team
Phase 3
Level 9+
Easier with boots — Q → E fast before she can Wall you off.
  • She's a terrible sidelaner — outperform in teamfights
  • Don't overcommit when egg is up

Look to get behind Anivia level 1 from the brushes and trade hard. If you can't hit the flip save your E to buffer through her Q stun and trade autos. Push her into the river to deny EXP. Be careful of committing Flash — it's really hard to kill the egg in time.

Wincon is making it to level 9 without falling behind. Very hard matchup — focus on farming and going even, then having more impact in 5v5s as a tankier bruiser. You can only play Q poke, no auto attacks. Only look to extend trades past Q if you bait out her Q stun first.

Very hard to trade — Q poke then disengage. Q → run at her → sidestep to bait panic Q. Keep your distance especially post-6 — she can Q → Wall → trap you in R and you lose 80% HP.

At level 9+ it becomes easier, especially with boots — Q → E her and fight fast before she can Wall you off. She's a terrible sidelaner. Don't overcommit when egg is up — disengage after popping egg.
