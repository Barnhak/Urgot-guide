Early game: You MUST fight Zed level 1! Get behind him and just trade. If he doesn't enter flip range, you can just E to gapclose and trade autos, push him into river/tower and deny EXP. Zed is very squishy so if you trade level 1, with E Flash W lvl 2 you can kill from ~70%HP.

How to trade: After early game, this matchup gets annoying as you can't E Zed (he just W's away) and he slowly pokes you down with Q. If you drop to ~50-60% HP in lane, try to find a way to reset. It's too hard to outplay Zed from that HP even if he has Ignite. If Zed doesn't want to die, it's hard to kill him, but he can only CS/poke with Q. Slow push/freeze waves and try to build a large CS/EXP lead. If he walks up to auto a minion, Q→PTA and trade hard.

After 6, don't E unless he has no W/shadow! He can easily dodge it or even R it. Try to E Flash only. Since Zed can R your E and your R, if you're all-inning without Flash, R from outside his ult range, then E behind yourself when he ults you. If he wastes W to poke, you will take damage, but pressure him after as he has to be scared of E flip now. If he takes enough damage, you can look to all in at ~60% HP with E Flash R. Need to 1shot during flip or else he can time out your R with his W/R.

What to watch out for: If Zed ults you, E behind yourself to guaranteed flip him → one shot. Pay attention to where his shadows are! You'll usually have to aim your E/R at the shadow he will swap back to instead of him, if you're not E Flash R one shotting him in the CC chain. His R can deny your R2, but the timing is very hard for Zed to pull off.

Tips: Don't force trades/fights, Zed is very hard to punish. Focus on Q poke, CS and outscaling in 1v1, sidelane, and 5v5 is a very good wincon.
