How to play VS

Early Game
Play aggro level 1
Get behind Ashe and fight level 1 — she's very squishy. Push her into river to deny EXP and trade autos.
  • Don't let her poke you down, respect her range if Flash is down
  • Take the bushes — she can't free-auto you there
How to trade
Q poke only
Her auto range is too high to trade autos for long. Go 3 points Q with triple tonic, then Q → Auto/Leg for poke, disengage.
  • Avoid W poke/autos when your Q is on CD
  • Stand behind minions to avoid her W
Kill Window
E Flash + Ignite/R
She's so squishy you can just E Flash to oneshot with Ignite/R from 90-100% HP.
  • Don't try to overforce pre-9 if Flash is down
  • After 9 with boots: E to gapclose → Q slow → run her down with R slow

Early game: You definitely need to try to get behind Ashe and fight level 1. Try to push her into river to deny EXP and trade autos. She has higher auto range but is super squishy, so don't be afraid to just E Flash early to chunk her and start fighting.

If you can't cheese, take the bush early to avoid her autos, then try to play for level 2 prio. You can Flash on her from basically full HP with E + W + Ignite.

How to trade: Ashe has super high auto range so you can only really trade with Q poke before she exits your range. Go 3 points Q with triple tonic, and once you get rank 2-3 Q you can Q → Auto Attack, especially if you have boots. If she's still in range turn on W to proc PTA, then disengage.

Avoiding W poke/autos when your Q is on cooldown is very important — if she can perma auto you, you'll get poked down very quickly. Take the bushes and stand behind minions to avoid this!

Extremely squishy so from 90-100% HP you can just send E Flash to oneshot with Ignite/R. Having Flash up is super important, or else you can't really touch Ashe. Always be looking to E Flash if she enters range, and respect when it's down.

What to watch out for: Main thing is just not committing your E unless you know it will hit (usually with E Flash), as she can run you down with her W + auto slows. Generally just don't try to E at her unless you have a lot of points in Q (more slow) or more MS (boots) or Flash up.

Tips: Steelcaps rush is really strong, especially if jungle is also AD. Your base stats + Ignite are more than enough to kill her, you just need MS to enter range. Don't try to overforce pre-9, Ashe is a very cheesy pick and her range + auto slows can really hurt if you miss E.

Going even is winning, as they'll have Ashe top lane. Don't panic/think you need to fight perma — playing around your jungler is very OP vs ADCs top, ping for it when Ashe is humping you under tower.
