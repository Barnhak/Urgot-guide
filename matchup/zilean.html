Early game: Zilean is giga weak level 1, just E at him to gapclose if he doesn't enter flip range and trade autos. Keep him pushed away until he loses EXP and keep trying to zone him. Really important you abuse early game, once he gets his E speed/slow it's hard to trade.

How to trade: Stand to the side of the lane to make him choose between Q'ing you or the wave. Can extend Q→W PTA trades if he misses Q. If he has Q up respect his double bomb range, usually you never E aggressively. E buffer away from his double bomb.

After lvl 3, it gets really difficult to trade with Zilean as he will just stand back and throw bombs and not enter E range. Try to approach him from the side of the lane and bait out a bomb, if he misses you can Q→Auto or even Q→W PTA if he wastes more cooldowns than you. At least make him choose between bombing you, or bombing the wave. Do not just walk into his double bomb range though, you will take too much damage. You can look for a Q, but anything more requires more wasted cooldowns. If he does double bomb you, E buffer is mostly just ends up being a free farming matchup that's fine. Focus on CS and not dying to jungle ganks.

After level 6, focus on farming and outscaling in the sidelane. You CAN kill after 6. That being said, you CAN kill after 6. E Flash and hold your R for a little bit until he ults. His ult will last 5s before it times out, so if you can let your R sit in him for 4s and ~1s to pull him in, you can time out his Ult if you play well. You can also just detonate his R, but you need to have either E Flip or R up after he revives to kill. DO NOT use both just to pop his ult.

What to watch out for: Watch the range of the bombs, if Zilean double bombs a minion it can stun/dmg you if you're close enough. Zilean can R himself during CC, so you can't E Flash R 1shot him usually. Don't get baited into going over aggressive, he's so hard to kill, and it just opens you up to getting ganked easily if you commit E.

Tips: Going even is fine, as you turbo outscale in the sidelane later on. Zilean is basically a support utility bot after lane. After 9, if Zilean is not interacting, look to proxy → jungle camps if you see enemy jungle on the opposite side. You start to farm way faster than him + he can't facecheck you so you should have perma prio and way more farm.
