Early game: Look to get behind Naafiri and trade level 1, after level 2 and 3 she becomes much harder to force a fight with as she gets dash + invuln. However if she respects the cheese and throws Q at you as you approach (doesn't facecheck), try to sidestep a Q before engaging. If you can't dodge it, you can E thru the Q and just auto, try to push her off the first 3 exp/CS since this won't result in that good of a HP trade.

How to trade: After level 3, never try to walk up and auto attack Naafiri as her Q outranges you if it's up, and she can dash (E) or invuln (W) your E. Play around your Q + Comet cooldown — Comet cooks Naafiri since she's melee, she usually has to enter Q range to CS, and then dash away from your Q giving you windows to abuse her cooldowns.

If she's poked down to ~50%HP, you can look for E + Flash + R with the +18AD triple tonic pot at level 6. Most Naafiris can't react with their W on in time for your E + Flash, but if you're in higher elo try to at least bait out the dash before doing this.

With our Q comet playstyle, Naafiri will have to use Q to CS, giving you a small window where you can look to weave in autos. Of course if she ever wastes W & E and you somehow are in flip range, you can look to flip, but this basically can never happen in lane.

Play patient with your Q poke, focus on growing a big CS lead since kills are hard, and hard outscale her in sidelane/5v5 later on.

What to watch out for: Never E if she has E or W up, her dash is a lower CD and faster cast time than our E. If you don't lock on to Naafiri with an ability, your W will hit her passive dogs instead (0 dmg to Naafiri), so be careful of this when trading — you need to lock on with Q to trade harder. Make sure you're max range Q'ing, if you Q too close to her she can dash over your Q and hit you with the E damage into a trade.

Tips: You can E Naafiri in her Ulti dash animation to knock it down. This lane is only hard if you try to punish beyond just Q poke. Focus on slow pushing waves, Q'ing her if she walks up, forcing her to CS with Q because of that (and growing a CS lead), and taking plates when she has to take a bad base. You turbo outscale in 1v1/5v5 so going even is winning.
