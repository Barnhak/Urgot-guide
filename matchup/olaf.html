Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand. And in secondary runes, Nimbus Cloak and Celerity — you need a lot of MS in this matchup and it's way better than Triple Tonic + Approach Velocity.

Summoners spells

or

Go either Ghost or Ignite. Ignite is really good for the anti heal and damage but I prefer to run Ghost and abuse Nimbus Cloak to hard tempo Olaf R and kill him after that.

How to play VS

Olaf level 1 is no joke — it's better than Urgot but only if he can get you. So try to proc PTA and if possible E him BUT NEVER go past the middle of the lane. From there at any level if you take an axe you are 100% dead. If you run Ignite you can first kill him level 1 or level 2 if you take an early trade. And if you manage to kill him level 1-3 it makes your lane way easier.

If you struggle you can first buy Bramble Vest — it makes your lane way easier and Olaf healing is part of his strength in short trade so if you cancel that you are stronger in short trade.

Abuse your pre-6 power — you are better than him until he procs Conq. But remember never to go past the middle of lane, and never commit your E if he's not around 65% HP or you might die. Olaf will try to poke you down with his Q from the side to force him to choose between you and the wave and zone him from his axe. When he wastes it you can Q + extend trade and he can't really gap close and win without his Q. Olaf E deals a lot of damage but both ways — if he doesn't kill you in his all in you can abuse that and Flash E + R to finish him.

After 6 never interact with Olaf or he will hard commit with Ghost R and run you down. If he does and you have a Ghost try matching them with Celerity and Nimbus Cloak — you outspeed him and you can make him waste his R. When in R Olaf wastes your R2 / E — that's why this lane is so hard, and his immune to slow so your Q is no help either. So after level 6 try playing the best you can with your range and sidestep his Q + take bush to play with his vision and keep your R for after his R — you have the same CD.

You can play for mana sustain and lower Olaf mana with his spam Q or W before engaging. Olaf R costs 100 mana which for him is a lot (at level 10 Olaf is around 700 max mana).

In late vs Olaf it's the better player who wins, and in team fight you are better but your team (especially your ADC) will not care and not kite enough Olaf so try helping them despite everything.
