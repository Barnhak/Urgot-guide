How to play VS

Phase 1
Level 1–3
Be aggressive level 1 before he stacks passive. E buffer his E stun.
  • Get level 2 prio with your range
  • Secure early HP advantage
Phase 2
Level 4–8
After level 3 just farm — he statchecks you with W healing.
  • E buffer away from his Q before it connects
  • Never let him get W2 proc — lane-ending heal
Phase 3
Level 9+
Post-9 with BC + anti heal you win 1v1. His R cannot block R2.
  • Anti heal is mandatory before fighting back
  • His R no longer blocks R2 anymore

Look to get behind him and fight level 1, after you auto him start sidestepping to dodge his E then look to E → Auto. Use this trade and your range advantage to secure level 2 prio. Do not let him auto the wave for free level 1 or he can stack his passive.

After level 3 just farm until 9, Volibear is a huge lane bully that omega statchecks you. If you take one bad trade he can easily dive you with his R. Let the wave push in and farm. If he runs at you with Q, buffer E away before the stun connects to avoid the combo + E damage. After level 6 if you E away don't walk back into his range because he can just R on you. Unless you can flip him into tower range never E forwards until 9. Make sure to wait for his W mark to time out, getting hit by one second W is lane ending.

Pay attention to the W mark, never let him get W2 off. Disengage and wait for it to expire and if he's low enough you can try to kill with R. Thankfully Volibears usually auto push to stack his passive so just use your range, don't let him walk up and auto/W you for free, use Q to space/slow him and focus on letting the wave push to you.

You need 1st item + Executioner's if you don't have Ignite to even think about fighting back. You can win 1v1 at 9+ with Black Cleaver/Executioner's and you hard outscale as the game goes on. Volibear's R cannot block R2 anymore.
