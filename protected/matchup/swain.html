Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand Cut Down. And in secondary runes, Triple Tonic and Approach Velocity to gap close and have kill pressure. Early AD is strong — you need to one shot him before he pokes you down.

Summoners spells

or

Ignite is recommended for kill pressure and anti heal after your first item. Ghost is also viable — don't buy early anti heal (especially with Ignite), it's not needed. Get it after your first item though.

How to play VS

Look to get behind Swain and fight — he's very weak early game and squishy. Wincon is poking Swain down with Q/Autos while dodging his E/Q poke until you can E + Flash one shot. If he pokes you down too much it's hard. Dance around his E (root) range and try to bait it out — if he does, look for Q → PTA or E → Q and hard trade. If he has E up, respect his trading power.

If Swain has E up you have to respect his range — wiggle around its max range and if you can bait it out then look for Q → PTA trade or Q → E if you can get close enough. If you E buffer as Swain E hits you, you can get closer to him to trade and deny his passive yoink + the stack. Swain is really squishy especially early game — if you get one good trade he's in lethal range so don't be afraid to E + Flash around 70% HP. He doesn't have good tools to peel himself if you get on top of him. If you E buffer right as Swain E roots you, you can prevent him from yoinking you and getting his passive stacks.

You actually have kill potential pre-9 in this matchup especially if you get a lead. After 9 you smoke him if you can close the gap as you out-DPS him even in ult form. His level 6 spike is not that strong in this lane — you can usually one shot on E + Flash before he can cast it, and it doesn't heal that much early on either. Don't overrespect his level 6, he's still very killable.

Swain's wincon is Q poke or E → Q → W comboing you enough until you're too low to threaten the all-in. Give extra care to not tank his abilities for free and make sure you punish them when they're on CD. Swain's scaling is pretty decent so it's important not to fall behind in this lane. Don't lose your Flash — you'll usually have to E + Flash to kill Swain, so if you lose it to a gank Swain can poke you hard without fear.
