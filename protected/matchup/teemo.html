Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand. And in secondary runes, Triple Tonic and Approach Velocity. Doran's Shield start is strong — it gives more room for mistakes against his poke.

Summoners spells

or

Ignite is the safest — you need kill pressure to E + Flash + Ignite + R oneshot. Ghost is also viable for more mobility to chase him down.

How to play VS

Doran's Shield start is strong, Doran's Blade is also OK but Doran's Shield gives more room for mistakes. Teemo has really high auto range so it's unlikely you can flip level 1. As the minions crash take the autos, when he autos E for shield → Auto → Disengage. If Teemo starts blind (Q) his AA damage is weak and it's hard for him to push.

Wincon is poking with Q while dodging his auto/Q poke until you can E + Flash + R when he's around 70% HP. Respect the blind in timing your R out. If Teemo uses his blind (Q) you can look to take a heavier Q → PTA trade. Don't flip early if he's full HP — chunk him first or he can win the all-in with blind + Ignite. If Teemo moves too close you can Q → E if he's under 60-70% HP. Teemo will always die if you hit Flip (E) but he's very fast with a small hitbox — be patient, slowly Q poke him down then E + Flash, or Q → E → R.

Try to keep a ward in the bush closest to your tower so you can see if he goes invisible there to cheese after the wave crashes. E/Ignite does not reveal Teemo if he's invisible in bush — if you slow first → E then he gets revealed. Merc Treads rush is strong — the extra movement speed helps combining Q → E and Tenacity reduces blind duration. Swap to Red Trinket at level 9 to clear shrooms.

Annoying champ in lane but he falls off hard — you are much stronger in sidelane/teamfights if not behind.
