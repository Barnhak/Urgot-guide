Runes & Summoners

Runes

Like always on Urgot you go PTA, Triumph and Bloodline and Last Stand. And in secondary runes, Triple Tonic and Approach Velocity.

Summoners spells

or

Ignite is better here than Ghost. Nasus slow is crazy and Ghost won't save you from it so better killing power and anti heal in Nasus passive is great. But you can run Ghost if you prefer to play for mid/late game.

How to play VS

Abuse Nasus pre-6 — he's literally not a champion before that and try zoning him the best you can. Nasus needs to have access to the wave in order to become a menace BUT he has 0 mana so if you zone him he will have to farm with E and waste his mana, forcing him to choose between you and the wave. In total Nasus E deals like 50 to 200 magic damage at impact and around 50-200 in the 5s it's active so you could not care, but the armor shred is important to think about.

Fight him level 1 and proceed to crash the 3rd wave and freeze the push back under your tower. If you chunk 30% of his HP you can kill him level 2 — Nasus has no stat early. Try either wasting his summs or XP. If you play the wave properly you can just perma freeze and when he backs fast push so he loses everything.

Before 6 try fighting as hard as possible — you win every fight even after 6 until he stacks enough. But after level 6 his zone deals a lot of damage and he gets a lot of HP bonus so try to all in at 60% HP. After 6 don't force too much fight from here — Nasus starts having crazy sustain with his life steal and just continue freezing and making a CS lead. Remember when fighting Nasus at every level: if he W you buffer your E in him so the slow will disappear very shortly after your CC on Nasus and never quit W — his attack speed doesn't affect your W.

After lane Nasus can outscale in 1v1 but if you did your job he will be so behind you can just run him down. But watch out for his stack before engaging. In team fight Nasus will just run on your ADC so the best is to CC him and R him. Except running down one person he won't do much.
